# Data Retention And Deletion

Last updated: 19 June 2026

This is a starter policy. It must be reviewed whenever the email provider, domain, or client workflow changes.

## Data Collected

- Enquiry name
- Email address
- Phone number if provided
- Service interest
- Location
- Message content
- Technical logs from Vercel, Resend, Zoho Mail, and optional Cloudflare Turnstile

## Sensitive Information

Visitors should not submit medical records, diagnosis details, financial information, identity documents, or highly sensitive personal information through the contact form.

For hoarding-related enquiries, only practical details should be requested, such as:

- the type of support needed
- location
- urgency
- preferred contact method
- whether a family member, carer, or support worker may be involved

## Suggested Retention

- Spam or irrelevant enquiries: delete as soon as practical.
- General enquiries that do not become clients: keep for up to 12 months.
- Active client communication: keep for the duration of the service relationship plus a defined business period.
- Sensitive unnecessary details: delete or minimise as soon as practical.

## Deletion Requests

Visitors can request deletion by contacting the business email address:

```text
info@heartyhome.co.uk
```

The business owner should check:

- business inbox
- Resend logs
- Zoho Mail logs and mailbox folders
- Vercel logs
- any future CRM, spreadsheet, or booking system
- backups where practical
